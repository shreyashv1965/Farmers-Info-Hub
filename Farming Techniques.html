Farming Techniques

1. 🇮🇳 Indian Farming Techniques

  • Rice Paddy Cultivation: Flooded‑field rice cultivation.
  • Wheat Farming: Major Rabi crop in northern India.
  • Indian Farmer Practices: Combination of traditional and modern methods.
  • Irrigation Systems: Canal, drip, and sprinkler irrigation.
  • Organic Farming in India: Eco‑friendly agricultural approach.
  • Ginger Farming: Important spice crop grown in India.

2. Organic Farming Techniques

  • Crop Rotation: Alternating crops to improve soil fertility.
  • Green Manure: Growing plants to enrich soil nutrients naturally.
  • Biofertilizers: Natural microorganisms improving soil health.
  • Composting: Recycling organic waste into nutrient‑rich manure.

3. Traditional Farming Techniques

  • Mixed Farming: Crops and livestock managed together.
  • Terrace Farming: Step‑wise farming on hills to reduce erosion.
  • Manual Farming: Farming using traditional tools and labor.
